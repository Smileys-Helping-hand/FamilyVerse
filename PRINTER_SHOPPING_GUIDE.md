## â“ FAQ

**Q: Will any thermal printer work?**  
A: Most will! Just need 50mm+ width and web browser compatibility.

**Q: Do I need special software?**  
A: No! FamilyVerse QR Studio works in your web browser.

**Q: Can I print from my phone?**  
A: Yes! If printer supports mobile, open QR Studio on phone.

**Q: How long do labels last?**  
A: Thermal labels last years if kept dry and out of direct sun.

**Q: Can I use colored labels?**  
A: Thermal printers only print black. But you can buy colored label paper!

**Q: Are labels reusable?**  
A: No, adhesive is one-time use. But that's why they're cheap!

**Q: What if my printer is different size?**  
A: Just adjust the width/height in QR Studio settings!

**Q: Can I share one printer with multiple parties?**  
A: Absolutely! Each party generates unique QR codes.
